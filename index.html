Our Story

Three Generations of Excellence

Valley Modern Plumbing & Hardware was founded by Genaro Hernandez Sr. right here in McAllen. Over the decades, the business evolved to meet the community's needs, growing into a trusted residential and commercial plumbing operation.

Today, Genaro Hernandez Jr. and his son Andrew carry that legacy forward — the same craftsmanship, the same commitment to quality, and the same respect for your home that built this business from the ground up.

We're not a call center or a franchise. When you call us, you reach a family that treats your home like their own. That's the kind of trust that only comes from three generations of doing the job right.
