Common Questions

Commercial Plumbing FAQ

Do you handle restaurant plumbing in McAllen?

Yes. Restaurant plumbing is one of our core commercial services. We handle grease trap installation and maintenance, commercial dishwasher hookups, gas line connections for cooking equipment, floor drain systems, and backflow prevention — all to health department code.

Can you do plumbing for a new commercial buildout?

Absolutely. We work with general contractors and property owners on new construction and tenant improvement buildouts throughout McAllen and the RGV. We handle rough-in, finish plumbing, fixture installation, and final inspection coordination.

Do you offer commercial plumbing service contracts?

Yes. We offer ongoing service contracts for commercial properties that include scheduled maintenance, priority emergency response, and preventive inspections. This keeps your business running without surprise plumbing emergencies.

Are you licensed for commercial plumbing in Texas?

Yes. Valley Modern Plumbing holds Texas Master Plumber License #PM12745, which covers both residential and commercial work. We are fully licensed, insured, and bonded for commercial projects of any size.

How fast can you respond to a commercial plumbing emergency?

For service contract clients, we offer same-day priority response. For new commercial clients, we typically respond within 24 hours for non-emergency calls and same-day for urgent situations like sewage backups, gas leaks, or water main breaks.
